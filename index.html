Who Are We?

The right arm of the gospel

What we believe about Medical Missionary Work

Again and again I have been instructed that the medical missionary work is to bear the same relation to the work of the third angel’s message that the arm and hand bear to the body. Under the direction of the divine Head they are to work unitedly in preparing the way for the coming of Christ. (Ellen G. White, Testimonies to the Church, Vol. 6, p. 288)

God will strengthen it

God's support of the Medical Missionary work

The right arm of the body of truth is to be constantly active, constantly at work, and God will strengthen it. But it is not to be made the body. At the same time the body is not to say to the arm: “I have no need of thee.” The body has need of the arm in order to do active, aggressive work. Both have their appointed work, and each will suffer great loss if worked independently of the other. (Ellen G. White, Testimonies to the Church, Vol. 6, p. 288)

Should we respect it?

God's respect for medical missionary work

The gospel of health has able advocates, but their work has been made very hard because so many ministers, presidents of conferences, and others in positions of influence have failed to give the question of health reform its proper attention. They have not recognized it in its relation to the work of the message as the right arm of the body. While very little respect has been shown to this department by many of the people and by some of the ministers, the Lord has shown His regard for it by giving it abundant prosperity. (Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church. Vol 6, p. 327 (1900))

Is it the Gospel?

Medical missionary work as only an open door for the gospel

But while the health work has its place in the promulgation of the third angel’s message, its advocates must not in any way strive to make it take the place of the message. (Ellen G. White, Testimonies for the Church. Vol 6, p. 327 (1900))

Our Lessons

Anatomy & Physiology

Here we teach our students on the structure and functions of the human body and disease related to this field

Disease and Management

We teach our students on the principles of disease, and their causes, how to treat them and how to manage them

Clinical Nutrition Therapy

Here we train our students on how to use food in healing and how to create programs that will help them in finding solutions for different diseases

Bible Study and Prophecy

Here we teach our students on how to hold bible studies, giving them a foundation on bible principles and absolutes

Agriculture

Here our students get the chance to get lessons on organic farming and essentials in farm keeping

Herbs and natural remedies

Here the student is taught on herbal administration and applications of natural remedies in cure for disease
