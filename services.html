Our Lessons

Anatomy & Physiology

Here we teach our students on the structure and functions of the human body and disease related to this field

Disease and Management

We teach our students on the principles of disease, and their causes, how to treat them and how to manage them

Clinical Nutrition Therapy

Here we train our students on how to use food in healing and how to create programs that will help them in finding solutions for different diseases

Bible Study and Prophecy

Here we teach our students on how to hold bible studies, giving them a foundation on bible principles and absolutes

Agriculture

Here our students get the chance to get lessons on organic farming and essentials in farm keeping

Herbs and natural remedies

Here the student is taught on herbal administration and applications of natural remedies in cure for disease
